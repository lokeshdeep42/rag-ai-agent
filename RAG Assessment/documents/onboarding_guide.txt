1. WELCOME AND OVERVIEW
========================

Congratulations on joining our team! We're excited to have you on board.

This guide will help you navigate your first days, weeks, and months at the company. We've designed a comprehensive onboarding program to ensure you have everything you need to succeed.

1.1 Onboarding Timeline
------------------------
- Day 1: Orientation, IT setup, workspace setup
- Week 1: Team introductions, initial training, system access
- Week 2-4: Role-specific training, project assignments
- Month 1: Check-in with manager, feedback session
- Month 3: Performance review, goal setting

1.2 Your Onboarding Buddy
--------------------------
You'll be assigned an onboarding buddy - a peer who will help you navigate the company, answer questions, and provide support during your first few months. Your buddy will reach out to you before your start date.

2. BEFORE YOUR FIRST DAY
=========================

2.1 Pre-Boarding Checklist
---------------------------
You should have received the following via email:

□ Welcome email from HR
□ Employment contract (signed and returned)
□ Benefits enrollment information
□ IT equipment shipping notification (if remote)
□ First day schedule and logistics
□ New hire paperwork link

If you haven't received these, contact HR at hr@company.com

2.2 Documents to Bring (First Day)
-----------------------------------
Please bring the following documents:

□ Government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport)
□ Social Security card or proof of eligibility to work
□ Bank account information (for direct deposit)
□ Emergency contact information
□ Completed tax forms (W-4)

2.3 What to Expect
------------------
- Start time: 9:00 AM
- Location: Main Office, Building A, Reception
- Dress code: Business casual (see employee handbook for details)
- Lunch: Provided on your first day
- End time: Approximately 5:00 PM

---

3. FIRST DAY CHECKLIST
=======================

3.1 Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
---------------------------------
□ Check in at reception
□ Meet with HR for orientation
  - Complete remaining paperwork
  - Review employee handbook
  - Enroll in benefits
  - Set up payroll and direct deposit
□ Receive access badge and building tour
□ IT equipment setup
  - Laptop/desktop computer
  - Phone (if applicable)
  - Access credentials
  - Email and calendar setup
□ Workspace setup

3.2 Afternoon (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
---------------------------------
□ Team lunch (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM)
□ Meet with your manager
  - Discuss role expectations
  - Review initial goals and objectives
  - Schedule regular 1-on-1 meetings
□ Meet your onboarding buddy
□ Department overview presentation
□ Initial security and compliance training
□ Set up essential accounts and tools

3.3 IT Account Setup
--------------------
You'll receive access to:

□ Email (Outlook/Office 365)
□ Slack (team communication)
□ HR Portal (benefits, time off, payroll)
□ Project management tools (Jira, Asana)
□ File storage (OneDrive, SharePoint)
□ VPN (for remote access)
□ Time tracking system
□ Learning management system

4. FIRST WEEK ACTIVITIES
=========================

4.1 Training Sessions
---------------------
Week 1 includes the following mandatory training:

□ Company overview and history
□ Products and services overview
□ Information security training
□ Compliance and ethics training
□ Diversity and inclusion training
□ Workplace safety training

All training is available in the Learning Management System (LMS).

4.2 Team Introductions
-----------------------
□ Meet with team members (scheduled by manager)
□ Attend team meetings
□ Join team Slack channels
□ Review team documentation and processes

4.3 System Access Verification
-------------------------------
Verify you have access to all necessary systems:

□ Email and calendar working
□ VPN connection successful
□ File storage accessible
□ Project management tools configured
□ Communication tools (Slack, Zoom) working
□ Department-specific applications

If you encounter any access issues, contact IT Help Desk:
- Email: helpdesk@company.com
- Phone: (555) 123-4567
- Portal: https://helpdesk.company.com

4.4 Key Meetings
----------------
□ Welcome meeting with department head
□ HR benefits overview session
□ IT security briefing
□ Facilities and office tour
□ Meet with cross-functional teams

5. FIRST MONTH MILESTONES
==========================

5.1 Week 2-3 Goals
------------------
□ Complete all mandatory training courses
□ Shadow team members on key processes
□ Begin working on initial projects/assignments
□ Attend relevant team and company meetings
□ Set up 1-on-1 meetings with key stakeholders
□ Review and understand team goals and OKRs

5.2 Week 4 Goals
----------------
□ 30-day check-in with manager
  - Discuss progress and feedback
  - Address any questions or concerns
  - Adjust onboarding plan if needed
□ Complete role-specific training
□ Take on more independent work
□ Contribute to team projects
□ Provide onboarding feedback to HR

5.3 End of Month 1
------------------
□ Submit onboarding feedback survey
□ Confirm benefits enrollment
□ Set performance goals with manager
□ Identify professional development opportunities
□ Schedule 90-day review

7. COMPANY CULTURE AND VALUES
==============================

7.1 Our Values
--------------

Innovation
- We embrace new ideas and technologies
- We encourage creative problem-solving
- We're not afraid to fail and learn

Collaboration
- We work together across teams and departments
- We value diverse perspectives
- We support each other's success

Integrity
- We do the right thing, even when it's difficult
- We're transparent in our communications
- We take responsibility for our actions

Customer Focus
- We put customers at the center of everything we do
- We strive to exceed expectations
- We listen and respond to feedback

Excellence
- We set high standards for ourselves
- We continuously improve our skills and processes
- We celebrate achievements and learn from setbacks

- Phone booths for private calls
- Cafeteria and break rooms

Dress Code
- Business casual
- Casual Fridays
